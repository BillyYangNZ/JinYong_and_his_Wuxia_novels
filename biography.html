Louis Cha (Chinese: 查良鏞; 6 February 1924 – 30 October 2018), better known by Jin Yong (Chinese: 金庸), was a Chinese wuxia ("martial arts and chivalry") novelist. He was named along with Gu Long and Liang Yusheng as the "Three Legs of the Tripod of Wuxia".


After Cha completed all his works, it was discovered that the first characters of the first 14 titles can be joined together to form a couplet with 7 characters on each line: Shooting a white deer, snow flutters around the skies; Smiling, [one] writes about the divine chivalrous one, leaning against bluish lovebirds (or lover).
